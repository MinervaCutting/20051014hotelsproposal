Hotel Melia Lebreros

The stylish Meliá Lebreros is located in Seville’s commercial district, 400 m from Santa Justa AVE Train Station. Renovated in 2016, it has a seasonal outdoor pool, gym and modern rooms with free Wi-Fi.

The hotel's smart rooms have air conditioning, a minibar and a private bathroom with free toiletries. The hotel also dedicates some of its areas to The Level exclusive rooms and services, where The Level guests have private access to The Level Lounge with a private buffet, daily cocktails, press and concierge services and pre-arrival services. Rooms include pillow menu, superior amenities and toiletries, sound tower, flat-screen TV with satellite channels, Nespresso coffee machine and turndown service, among other services.

Meliá Lebreros provides a variety of culinary options. Mosaico Restaurant offers an all-day dining experience and specialises in local and international cuisine. Guests can also enjoy local dishes in a traditional atmosphere at La Taberna la Dehesa or have a drink at Elyxr Bar.

The hotel's 4 restaurants include the Udaipur buffet restaurant and an à la carte Asian restaurant. There is also a Mediterranean and international restaurant, as well as 3 distinctive bars.
